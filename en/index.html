About us

The Beauty Parlour is a beauty and wellness space where you can find many services.

This project was born due to Sarah's interests for fashion, beauty and especially the aesthetics. By a young age, she started to do manicures, pedicures, make-ups and other beauty treatments to friends and relatives. Later she decided to raise this hobby to a professional level. After investing in many courses to become a professional manicure, pedicure and nail stylist, Sarah created the Beauty Parlour project, which is available to the public since 2015.

To be able to offer our clients an excellent service, we often invest in new revolutionary materials, workshops and trainings, this way we will have always available new services for our clients. Visit us and discover our services!
